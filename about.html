About Us

History

SourceSync Consulting, LLC, a dynamic, privately held engineering and consulting services company was founded in 2012. While headquartered in the bustling Fulton River District of Chicago, IL, we have strong relationships with industry colleagues and manufacturers worldwide. Having identified the need for US-based product management and cost-effective solutions, SourceSync Consulting has established effective partnerships with manufacturers who have proven success in demanding markets. Though incorporated in 2012, these relationships have been developed over years of industry experience.

Our founders are seasoned professionals and engineers with high exposure to diverse, cost-sensitive manufacturing. Today, our team includes engineers with industry experience in virtually every market including commercial, automotive, life sciences, government/military, aerospace, and consumer products. We believe our engineers and business development leaders’ ability to understand emerging market needs stems from having international resources working together as a team. Our partnerships span Asia, Europe, and the United States.

Our background and fundamental engineering expertise lies within the fabrication, development, and application of elastomeric-based products utilized in demanding environments. Understanding that every client’s needs are unique, our services span a wide range. Our engineers can help consult on a basic design and manufacturing feasibility. We can provide drawings, models, and data the very next day. We serve individuals searching for engineering support and large corporations looking to enhance supplier relations and global development.
