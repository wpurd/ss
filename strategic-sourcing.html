Competing in a Global Market Today

In the highly competitive global industrial market, it can be tough to find qualified suppliers in Low Cost Countries. It is no secret that sourcing from Low Cost Countries leverages companies with lower labor wages and production costs to bolster the bottom-line by reducing operating costs. However, if there is not an effective sourcing management strategy in place, the procurement process can cause headaches and present many pitfalls. In some cases, it is difficult to keep up with supplier relationship management due to language and cultural differences. In fact, sometimes international sourcing presents no value or cost savings. Here’s where SourceSync comes into play. We have the expertise and the knowledge to streamline the procurement process and successfully import products manufactured in Low Cost Countries. Most clients can realize up to 30-40% savings from their current purchasing situation. Our global network of suppliers undergo a rigorous pre-qualification process which minimizes relative risk. While we have a strong business acumen, we are fundamentally an Engineering focused company at our core. This technical approach not only guarantees cost savings, but a holistic analysis of our supply partners. With SourceSync’s services, working with customers thousands of miles away can be as straightforward as working with a supplier next door.

Plastic Components

By leveraging our expertise in the field of elastomeric materials, we are able to apply our knowledge to the world of plastics. More specifically, we work on a regular basis with engineered plastics which have ideal mechanical and/or thermal properties than commoditized plastic materials. Some of these materials range from acrylonitrile butadiene styrene (ABS) to polycarbonates. These types of materials have gained popularity in replacing materials such as wood or metal in many applications. Components are designed with weight/strength and other properties in mind while benefitting from the ease of manufacture. Current global market consumption of plastics in 2011 was estimated to be $45 billion in 2011 and expected to reach $76 billion by 2017. With extensive knowledge of innovated processes and years of experience in the field, SourceSync is able to find the most cost effective solution for any application and delivery quality products.

No matter the application or challenge, our engineers are ready to help you design and manufacture your plastic component needs.

Manufacturing Model Development

We examine the value chain of client's industry from an international perspective and formulate viable manufacturing options to meet client company's particular requirements. Cost savings and other critical financial variables are estimated under selected scenarios. Required supplier capability mix and performance standard are defined accordingly. Governance structures for the contract manufacturing relationship that maximize client company's strategic benefits are mapped out.

Manufacturer Identification and Capability Assessment

In identifying qualified supplier candidates in China, our bi-national professionals process large amount of company information in both English and Chinese languages in a short period of time. Through business intelligence, in-depth interviews and on-site evaluations, we assess candidates' manufacturing process, customer orientation, product development capability, quality culture, international experience, financial standing, and other aspects specified in the predetermined selection criteria. Supplier candidates that represent a strategic fit are recommended for client review.

Manufacturing Agreement Negotiation and Partnership Formation

SourceSync experts provide effective support to corporate executives in finalizing sourcing structure, pricing estimation, supplier incentive system and in negotiating the final manufacturing agreement to advance client company's interests. We also assist in clarifying pertinent regulatory conditions, gaining regulatory approval and completing registrations with government authorities when necessary.

Manufacturer Performance Evaluation and Project Management

After the manufacturing partnership is established, we provide professional assistance in conducting scheduled evaluations of supplier performance based on internal standard and industry best practice, and recommend concrete measures for continued improvement in supply chain performance. Client company also benefit from our international project management expertise in areas such as integration of management processes, cross-cultural communication, and supply chain resources coordination.
