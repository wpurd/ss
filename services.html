Rapid Prototyping

Whether building a 3D model to better visualize design concepts, impress your audience, prototype or manufacture your product, you have come to the right place. Rapid prototyping can help verify a design, communicate an idea and fix design issues early in the development process preventing costly changes to the hard tool once the product is in full production. A physical model also helps potential investors and customers better visualize the product at hand, leading to further investment in the product before it goes to market.

  • CNC Machining of Plastic and Metals
