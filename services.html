Materials & Processing

Typical manufacturing materials such as steel, aluminum, and other metals are readily available and well versed in the technical community – we can assist you with this as well. Conversely, Source Sync establishes our real expertise with our extensive knowledge of polymers. Our founders are established engineers who have spent a substantial portion of their corporate careers working with polymeric solutions for mechanical applications. We understand how your product’s operating conditions can be optimized by complex material development in ways that most of our industries industrial and mechanical engineers do not.

  • Elastomers (EPDM, FKM, VMQ, FVMQ, SBR, NR, CR, etc)
  • Co-polymers, Ter-polymers, Tetra-polymers
  • Thermoplastic Elastomers
  • Thermoplastics (PTFE, PEEK, UHMW, PE, PC, PA, PP, etc)
